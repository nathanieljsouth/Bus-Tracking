My bus tracker icon design

I wanted the bus icon to be easily recognisable, but found out very early on that users found a bus that was 'pointing' left or right to be very confusing as they thought the bus was travelling in the direction it was pointing. Therefore I chose a 'head-on' bus icon to avoid the direction of travel problem.

I had to make sure that the bus icon was transparent so it could work well with Google maps, and I also had to create a 'pin' shape so the exact location can be seen.
